Welcome to Shabbir and Sons

We are a leading mango exporter based in Pakistan, specializing in high-quality mangoes for the international market. Pakistan is one of the largest producers and exporters of mangoes in the world. Known for their sweet and juicy flavor, Pakistani mangoes are a popular choice among fruit lovers around the globe. The country produces a wide variety of mangoes, including Chaunsa, Sindhri, Anwar Ratol, and Langra, among others. Mango exporters from Pakistan take great pride in ensuring that their fruit is of the highest quality. They employ the latest techniques in cultivation, harvesting, and packaging to ensure that the mangoes are fresh, ripe, and ready for export. Additionally, they work closely with international standards organizations to ensure that their products meet all necessary quality and safety requirements. Overall, mango exporters from Pakistan are committed to delivering the best possible product to their customers around the world. They take great care in ensuring that each mango is of the highest quality and flavor, and they work tirelessly to maintain Pakistan's reputation as a leading producer and exporter of this beloved fruit.
